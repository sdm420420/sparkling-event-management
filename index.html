About Us

Our Story

Sparking Event Management Group Patna was founded in 2010 with a vision to create extraordinary events that leave lasting impressions. Over the past decade, we have grown from a small team of passionate event planners to one of Patna's most trusted event management companies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform your vision into reality through meticulous planning, creative design, and flawless execution. We believe every event is unique, and we dedicate ourselves to making each one unforgettable.
